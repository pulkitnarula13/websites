We Have 21 Years of
Experience

Group Security is a leading security company with over 21 years of experience in the Canadian market. Our mission is to provide top-notch security services to businesses, organizations and individuals across the country, and we've built a reputation as one of the most reliable and professional security companies in the industry.

We believe that security is not just about protecting property and assets, but also about ensuring the safety and well-being of our clients and their employees, customers, and visitors. That's why our team of experienced security professionals is dedicated to delivering the highest quality services, and our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering.

At Group Security, we understand that every client is unique and has different security needs, which is why we offer a range of customized security solutions to meet the specific requirements of each client. Whether you need security guards, mobile patrols, surveillance cameras, access control systems, or a combination of these services, we have the expertise and resources to provide a solution that meets your needs.

Our security personnel are highly trained and equipped with the latest technology, including body-worn cameras, mobile devices, and GPS tracking systems, to ensure that they have the tools they need to provide effective and efficient security services. Our security guards are also trained in conflict resolution, first aid, and other critical skills to ensure that they are able to handle any situation that may arise.

We believe that open communication is key to building a strong and lasting relationship with our clients, which is why we make it a priority to stay in close contact with each of our clients. Our security coordinators are available 24/7 to answer any questions or concerns you may have, and our management team is always available to meet with you in person to discuss your security needs.

At Group Security, we take great pride in our commitment to sustainability and social responsibility. We are constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental footprint, and we encourage our clients to do the same. We also believe in giving back to the communities we serve, and we are actively involved in local initiatives to support and enrich the lives of those in need.

In conclusion, Group Security is a trusted security company with over 21 years of experience in the Canadian market. Our team of dedicated and professional security personnel is committed to providing the highest quality security services to our clients, and our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering. If you're looking for a reliable and trustworthy security partner, look no further than Group Security.
